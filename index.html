Slider

Basic slider written in vanilla javascript

fadeIn

Fade slide in

slideIn[side]

Slide in from [side]

slideIn[verticalside][horizontalside]

Slide in from a corner

slideInHorizontal

If slide is upcoming in the line, slide in from right side, otherwise slide in from left (suffix with "Reversed" for opposit).

slideInVertical

If slide is upcoming in the line, slide in from top side, otherwise slide in from bottom (suffix with "Reversed" for opposit).
